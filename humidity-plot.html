Latitude vs Humidity



It is understandable for one assume that locations located nearer to the equator are more humid than locations further away (think the tropics). However, looking at the City Latitude vs. Humidity scatter plot above, there does not seem to be much releationship at all between the two.

That being said, one thing I can see in the data is that locations between 20 degrees north and 20 degrees south latitude don't seem to have a humidity of less than 20%.
