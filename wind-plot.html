Latitude vs Wind Speed



Looking at the City Latitude vs. Wind Speed scatter plot above, there does not seem to be a noticeable trend in the relationship between wind speeds and latitude, as the plot markers are distrubuted evenly across the full range of latitudes. While negative latitudes had no recorded wind speeds of greater than 20 mph, there is nothing in the data to indicate a relationship between latitude and wind speed.
